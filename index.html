Frequently Asked Questions

What types of manpower do you supply?

We provide skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled labor for government projects including security, housekeeping, drivers, and IT staff.

Do you assist with government tenders?

Yes! We offer end-to-end tender assistance, documentation, e-tendering, and vendor registration (GeM, PSU, MSME).

Can you handle solar energy projects?

Absolutely. We manage solar panel tenders, installation, O&M, rooftop solar, and rural electrification.

How do you ensure compliance in audits?

We follow strict audit protocols and use trained professionals to ensure transparency and accuracy.
